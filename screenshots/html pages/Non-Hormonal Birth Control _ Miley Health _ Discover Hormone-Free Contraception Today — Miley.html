Tired of Hormonal Side Effects?

For many women, hormonal birth control comes with tradeoffs. Mood changes, headaches, weight fluctuation, breast tenderness, and reduced libido are some of the reasons women begin searching for alternatives. These experiences can affect confidence, relationships, energy levels, and overall quality of life. For some women, the side effects simply outweigh the convenience of traditional hormonal contraception.

Other women are not necessarily unhappy with hormonal birth control. They simply want more flexibility and control over when and how contraception is used. Some prefer an option that is not taken daily or one that does not involve ongoing hormonal exposure. Wanting a birth control method that better fits your lifestyle and preferences is a valid and informed choice.

Important Safety Information

Rare cases (0.36%) of bladder and kidney infections have been reported. If you have a history of urinary tract infections that keep coming back, talk to your doctor about Phexxi.

Contact your healthcare provider if you are experiencing genitourinary side effects such as: vaginal burning, itching, dysuria, genital discomfort including vulvar or perineal pain, yeast infections, urinary tract infection or bacterial vaginosis.

Avoid Phexxi if you or your sexual partner is allergic to lactic acid, citric acid, potassium bitartrate, or any of the ingredients in Phexxi. Stop using Phexxi if you develop an allergic reaction.

Phexxi does not protect against sexually transmitted infections, including HIV. Avoid using Phexxi with a vaginal ring.
