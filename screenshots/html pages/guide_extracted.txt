### H1: What Is Phexxi?
Phexxi, now called Phexx, is the first and only hormone-free prescription birth control gel used on demand before sex. Unlike daily pills or long-term devices, Phexx is used only when needed, offering a flexible option for women exploring non-hormonal contraception.
This guide explains what Phexxi is, how it works, who it may suit, and how to get started.
### H1: Who Is It For?
Phexxi is an on-demand birth control method used right before intercourse. It works by maintaining vaginal pH in a way that makes it harder for sperm to move toward an egg.
This mechanism helps reduce the chances of pregnancy without using hormones.
Here is how Phexx works in practice:
It is inserted into the vagina using a prefilled applicator
It can be used up to 1 hour before intercourse
It starts working immediately after insertion
It does not work if used after sex
A new dose is required before each act of vaginal sex
Because Phexx is used only when needed, it may appeal to women who prefer flexibility over daily or long-term contraception.
Many women consider Phexx because:
There is no daily pill to remember
There is no long-term device
It is used only when needed
It is a hormone-free prescription option
This format offers a different approach compared to traditional birth control methods.
### H1: Who Is It For?
Phexxi may be a good option for women who want more flexibility and control over their contraception.
It may appeal to women looking for:
A hormone-free birth control option
An alternative to daily birth control pills
A non-permanent option instead of a long-term device
More flexibility around when birth control is used
A prescription method used right before sex
However, Phexxi is not right for everyone. Women with a history of recurrent urinary tract infections or certain urinary tract abnormalities may need to avoid it.
Before prescribing Phexx, a licensed Miley Health clinician reviews each patient’s consultation and medical history. This helps determine whether Phexx is appropriate for individual needs
### H1: How to Use Phexxi: A Step-by-Step Guide
Using Phexxi is straightforward. Follow these steps:
# H3: Step-by-Step Instructions
Wash your hands before use
Remove the prefilled applicator and plunger from the pouch
Insert the plunger into the applicator
Remove the cap from the applicator
Gently insert the applicator into the vagina as far as comfortable
Push the plunger to release the gel
Remove and discard the used applicator
# H3: Timing and Dosing Rules
Use one dose immediately before or up to 1 hour before sex
Phexx works right away after insertion
It does not work if used after sex
A new applicator is needed for each act of vaginal sex
If one hour passes before sex, use a new dose
These instructions help ensure Phexx is used effectively and as intended.
# H3: Frequently Asked Questions
What is Phexxi?
Phexxi, now called Phexx, is a hormone-free vaginal gel birth control used before intercourse. It is an on-demand contraceptive method rather than a daily pill or long-term device.
How does Phexxi work?
Phexxi works by maintaining vaginal pH in a way that makes it harder for sperm to move effectively toward an egg. It is used before intercourse and starts working immediately after insertion.
How do I use Phexxi?
Phexxi is inserted into the vagina using a prefilled applicator. One dose is used before intercourse, and a new dose is needed for each act of vaginal sex.
When do I use Phexxi?
Phexxi should be used immediately before or up to one hour before vaginal intercourse. If more than one hour passes, another dose is needed before sex.
How often do I need to apply Phexxi?
You should use one applicator before each act of vaginal intercourse. A new dose is required every time.
Is Phexxi hormone free?
Yes. Phexxi is a hormone-free birth control option. It does not contain estrogen or progestin.
Who is Phexxi best suited for?
Phexxi may be suited for women seeking non hormonal contraception, flexibility, and an alternative to daily or long-term birth control methods.
Does Phexxi protect against STIs?
No. Phexxi does not protect against sexually transmitted infections. Condoms may be used for additional protection.
Do I need a prescription for Phexxi?
Yes. Phexxi is a prescription birth control option. A licensed clinician reviews your medical history before prescribing.
Can I get Phexxi online?
Yes. You can complete an online birth control consultation through Miley Health. A licensed provider reviews your information and prescribes Phexxi if appropriate.

Important Safety Information
Rare cases (0.36%) of bladder and kidney infections have been reported. If you have a history of urinary tract infections that keep coming back, talk to your  doctor about Phexxi.
Contact your healthcare provider if you are experiencing genitourinary side effects such as: vaginal burning, itching, dysuria, genital discomfort including vulvar or perineal pain, yeast infections, urinary tract infection or bacterial vaginosis.
Avoid Phexxi if you or your sexual partner is allergic to lactic acid, citric acid, potassium bitartrate, or any of the ingredients in Phexxi. Stop using Phexxi if you develop an allergic reaction.
Phexxi does not protect against sexually transmitted infections, including HIV. Avoid using Phexxi with a vaginal ring.
