## H2: What is Phexx?
Phexx is a hormone-free birth control gel used right before sex. It works by maintaining vaginal pH levels that make it difficult for sperm to reach the egg.

## H2: How Phexx Works
Phexx is applied right before sex or up to 1 hour before.
1. Prep
Insert the plunger rod into the applicator and remove the cap.
2. Apply
Gently insert the applicator and press the plunger to release the gel.
3. Protect
Remove the applicator. Phexx works by maintaining vaginal pH levels that reduce sperm mobility.

What is Phexx?
Phexx is a prescription, hormone-free contraceptive gel used before sex.
Do I need a prescription?
Yes. A licensed provider reviews your consultation before prescribing.
How fast can I get Phexx?
Most prescriptions are reviewed within 24 hours.
Can I use Phexx with other types of birth control?
Phexx doesn't have to be used alone. You can use it with other contraceptives including condoms or diaphragms, but not with a vaginal ring.
Is Phexx hormone-free?
Yes. Phexx works locally without hormones.

Important Safety Information
Rare cases (0.36%) of bladder and kidney infections have been reported. If you have a history of urinary tract infections that keep coming back, talk to your  doctor about Phexxi.
Contact your healthcare provider if you are experiencing genitourinary side effects such as: vaginal burning, itching, dysuria, genital discomfort including vulvar or perineal pain, yeast infections, urinary tract infection or bacterial vaginosis.
Avoid Phexxi if you or your sexual partner is allergic to lactic acid, citric acid, potassium bitartrate, or any of the ingredients in Phexxi. Stop using Phexxi if you develop an allergic reaction.
Phexxi does not protect against sexually transmitted infections, including HIV. Avoid using Phexxi with a vaginal ring.
